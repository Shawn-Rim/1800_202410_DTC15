About WasteNot

Welcome to WasteNot, an app designed by Shawn, Jordan, and Alex, three students from the BCIT CST Diploma program.
Our mission is to revolutionize the way you manage your groceries. We understand that food waste is a pressing issue, and we’re here to help combat it. Our app not only helps you keep track of your groceries but also suggests mouth-watering recipes based on what you have on hand. This way, you can enjoy a variety of dishes and minimize food waste at the same time. We’ve poured our passion for technology and sustainability into this app, and we hope it brings convenience and inspiration to your culinary journey!

FAQ

What is the purpose of WasteNot?

The app is designed to help you organize your groceries and reduce food waste by suggesting recipes based on the ingredients you have.

How does the app reduce food waste?

The app reduces food waste by suggesting recipes that use the ingredients you already have in your pantry. This way, you can utilize your groceries before they go bad.

How does WasteNot help organize groceries?

WasteNot allows you to keep track of what groceries you have, how much you have, and when they expire. This helps you plan your meals and shopping trips more effectively.

Is the app free to use?

Yes, the app is free to use. We believe in making sustainable living accessible to everyone.
